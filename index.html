Datafilm notebook for film photographers

Record

Keep track of your aperture, shutter speed, compensation, flash, focal, lens, geolocation, date and time. Optionally attach a reference picture and a note to each frame.

Export

Instantly export and save your metadata to Exif, CSV or Text Document and share them to your other Apple devices or contacts.

More

Organize your library into folders. Add, delete, update and search films. Edit your frames anytime including parameters like geolocation and date.
